About Gulf Wasta

Connecting skilled workers with great opportunities since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by industry veterans, Gulf Wasta was born out of a need for better recruitment solutions in the blue collar sector. We saw too many skilled workers struggling to find good jobs and employers frustrated with unreliable staffing agencies.

Today, we're proud to be one of the nation's leading blue collar recruitment firms, with teams in mutiple countries and a network of over 5000 employers. Our success comes from our deep understanding of the trades and our commitment to treating every candidate with respect.

We don't just fill positions - we build lasting relationships that help workers grow their careers and businesses thrive.
